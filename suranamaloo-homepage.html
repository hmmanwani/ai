About Surana Maloo & Co.

We are a professionally managed and privately owned firm established in 1991 under the Chartered Accountants Act, 1949. Headquartered in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, our strong family tree with qualified and experienced professionals has extended our horizons into new domains.

Our vision is to provide excellent Auditing & Assurance, Direct, Indirect and International Tax Consultancy, Statutory Audit, FEMA/RBI, Legal Representations and Management Consultancy services to our client base.
