Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

What is your minimum order quantity?

We accommodate orders of all sizes, from small residential projects to large commercial developments. There's no strict minimum order quantity, but bulk orders receive better pricing.

Do you provide delivery services?

Yes, we provide reliable delivery services across Mumbai and surrounding areas. Delivery charges depend on location and order size. Free delivery is available for orders above certain thresholds.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfers, UPI, credit/debit cards, and cheques. For large orders, we can arrange flexible payment terms and credit facilities for established clients.

Do you offer technical consultation?

Absolutely! Our experienced team provides free technical consultation to help you choose the right materials for your project. We can also assist with quantity estimation and specifications.

What is your return and exchange policy?

We offer returns and exchanges for unused materials within 7 days of delivery, subject to our terms and conditions. Custom-ordered items may have different return policies.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive a tracking number via SMS and email. You can also call our customer service team for real-time updates on your order status.
