Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

What are your delivery options?

We offer free delivery within Ahmedabad city limits for orders above ₹10,000. For other locations, delivery charges are calculated based on distance and order value. We also provide installation services for most products.

Do you provide installation services?

Yes, we provide professional installation services for most of our products. Our team of experienced technicians ensures proper installation and quality workmanship. Installation charges vary based on the product and complexity.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day return policy for most products in their original packaging. Custom or made-to-order items are non-returnable. Damaged or defective products are replaced or refunded as per warranty terms.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes, we offer attractive bulk discounts for large orders. Discounts vary based on order value and product category. Contact our sales team for custom pricing and volume discounts.

Can I get samples before ordering?

Yes, we provide samples for most products. Sample charges are nominal and are adjusted against your final order. Visit our showroom or contact us to request samples.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, card payments, UPI, net banking, and all major digital payment methods. We also offer EMI options for orders above ₹5,000 through our partner banks.
