About Truvio

Our Story

The journey that brought us here

Building Dreams, One Space at a Time

Founded in 2020, Truvio emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to make quality interior materials accessible to everyone. What started as a small family business in Ahmedabad has grown into a comprehensive solution provider for all home interior needs.

Our founders, with decades of experience in the construction and interior design industry, recognized the challenges homeowners and professionals faced in sourcing reliable, high-quality materials. They saw the need for a one-stop destination that could offer everything from electrical fittings to premium flooring solutions.

Today, Truvio stands as a testament to our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. We've helped thousands of customers transform their spaces, from cozy apartments to luxurious villas, always maintaining the highest standards of product quality and service excellence.
