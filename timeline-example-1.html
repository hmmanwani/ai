Our Journey

Three decades of professional excellence

1991

Foundation Established

Surana Maloo & Co. was established under the Chartered Accountants Act, 1949, marking the beginning of our professional journey in Ahmedabad, Gujarat.

1995

Expansion of Services

Extended our services to include comprehensive auditing and assurance services, building trust with our growing client base.

2000

Tax Advisory Excellence

Developed specialized expertise in direct and indirect taxation, becoming a trusted partner for tax planning and compliance.

2005

Corporate Finance Division

Launched our corporate finance advisory services, helping businesses with project financing and strategic financial planning.

2010

International Services

Expanded into international tax consultancy and transfer pricing solutions, serving multinational corporations.

2015

Digital Transformation

Embraced technology and digital solutions to enhance our service delivery and client experience.

2020

Pandemic Resilience

Successfully navigated the challenges of the global pandemic while maintaining service excellence and client support.

2024

Future Forward

Continuing our legacy of excellence with innovative solutions and unwavering commitment to client success.
